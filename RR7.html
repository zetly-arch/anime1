3.Hanagaki Takemichi

Hanagaki Takemichi is the main protagonist of the Tokyo Revengers series. He is a 26-year boy who discovers he can travel back in time to his middle school days. Takemichi is determined to save his ex-girlfriend, Hinata Tachibana, and change the future by altering the events of his past. Despite his initial cowardice, he grows into a courageous and determined individual, willing to face powerful enemies and make sacrifices for those he cares about.


4.Mikey

Mikey, also known as Manjiro Sano, is the leader of the Tokyo Manji Gang (Toman) and one of the central characters in Tokyo Revengers. He is a charismatic and powerful figure with a strong sense of loyalty to his friends. Despite his tough exterior, Mikey has a deep sense of justice and cares deeply for his gang members. His relationship with Takemichi is complex, as they share a bond that transcends time and challenges.


5.Baji Keisuke

Baji Keisuke is a key member of the Tokyo Manji Gang and one of Takemichi's closest friends. He is known for his fierce loyalty and strong sense of justice. Baji plays a crucial role in the story, particularly in the events leading up to the conflict with the rival gang, Valhalla. His character embodies the themes of friendship, sacrifice, and the struggles of youth.
