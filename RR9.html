9. Satoru Gojo

Satoru Gojo is a prominent character in the anime and manga series "Jujutsu Kaisen." He is a powerful jujutsu sorcerer and serves as a teacher at Jujutsu High School. Known for his striking white hair and blindfold, Gojo possesses immense strength and skill in combat. He is often seen as a mentor to the main protagonist, Yuji Itadori, and plays a crucial role in the fight against curses. Gojo's character is characterized by his laid-back attitude, confidence, and unwavering dedication to protecting humanity from supernatural threats.


Gojo's abilities include the Six Eyes, which grants him exceptional perception and control over cursed energy, making him one of the most formidable jujutsu sorcerers in the series. His relationship with other characters, such as Megumi Fushiguro and Nobara Kugisaki, showcases his protective nature and willingness to go to great lengths to ensure their safety. Gojo's character development throughout the series highlights his struggles with the responsibilities of being a powerful sorcerer and his desire to create a better world for future generations.


11. Itadori Yuji

Itadori Yuji is the main protagonist of "Jujutsu Kaisen." He is a high school student who becomes involved in the world of jujutsu sorcery after consuming a cursed object, Ryomen Sukuna's finger. Despite his initial reluctance, Yuji embraces his role as a jujutsu sorcerer to protect others from curses and seeks to find a way to exorcise Sukuna from his body. His character is defined by his strong sense of justice, compassion, and determination to save people, even at great personal risk.


Yuji's journey is marked by his growth as a sorcerer, his relationships with fellow jujutsu sorcerers, and his internal struggle with the curse of Sukuna. He forms close bonds with characters like Megumi Fushiguro and Nobara Kugisaki, who become his allies in the fight against curses. Yuji's character development explores themes of sacrifice, the value of life, and the complexities of good and evil in the world of jujutsu sorcery.
