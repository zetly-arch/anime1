8.Sung Jin-Woo

Sung Jin-Woo is the main protagonist of the "Solo Leveling" series, a South Korean web novel written by Chugong. Initially introduced as an E-rank hunter, he is often referred to as the "World's Weakest Hunter". However, after a near-fatal encounter in a double dungeon, he gains the unique ability to level up like a character in a video game. This transformation allows him to become one of the most powerful hunters in the world. His journey is marked by his relentless pursuit of strength, his desire to protect his loved ones, and his evolution from an underdog to a formidable force against various threats.


The series explores themes of growth, perseverance, and the consequences of power. For fans of action-packed fantasy and character development, "Solo Leveling" offers a compelling narrative with stunning illustrations and a gripping storyline. To read more about Sung Jin-Woo and his adventures, you can find the web novel on platforms like KakaoPage or Tapas, and the manhwa adaptation is available on various manga reading sites.
