7.Iruma Suzuki

Iruma Suzuki is the main protagonist of the anime and manga series "Welcome to Demon School! Iruma-kun." He is a mortal who is sold to a demon by his parents and ends up attending a demon school called Babyls. Despite being human, Iruma possesses a kind heart and a strong sense of justice, which endears him to his demon classmates. Iruma is known for his ability to adapt to the demon world, often finding himself in humorous and challenging situations. His character is characterized by his blue hair, green eyes, and a distinctive school uniform. Throughout the series, Iruma navigates the challenges of being a human in a demon school, forming friendships and alliances with various demons. His journey is filled with comedic moments, as he tries to keep his true identity a secret while also proving himself in the demon world.


Iruma's character development is a central theme of the series, as he learns to embrace his unique position and the friendships he forms with his demon classmates. His interactions with characters like Clara Valac, Asmodeus Alice, and the school's principal, Sullivan, highlight his growth and adaptability. Iruma's story is one of self-discovery, friendship, and the challenges of fitting into a world that is vastly different from his own. And he is so powerful that he kept his teacher as his summouning pet. He is the demon king's grandson and has the ability to summon powerful demons, which adds to his unique status in the demon world.
